About me

Hello! I am Aditya Kartikeya. I am pursuing my Master’s in Data Science from Indiana University Bloomington and I have bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communications engineering from NIT Warangal. I have a big interest in how data shapes our lives. Data helps us make our decisions easier because it is backed by evidence. This is the reason why I am in this exciting field. I read a lot about different ways of manipulating data to our needs and keep learning latest trends and technologies in this field. I believe Data Visualization is a very key aspect of data science. During my leisure time I like to read about stock market trading, technical analysis, HFT and Hedge funds. I am also very interested in algorithmic trading. Trading is something that gives me thrill because your analysis yields you results and how well you have understood the data leads to your success.
